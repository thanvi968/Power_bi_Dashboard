# Data Jobs Dashboard v2 w/ Power BI

![Dashboard Page1](/images/Project2_Page1.PNG)

## Introduction

Navigating the data job market can feel like a maze with information scattered everywhere. This dashboard (v2.0) is designed specifically for **Job Seekers, Job Trasitions, and Job Swappers** to cut through the clutter! Using a real-world dataset of 2024 data science job posting-rich with deatils on titles, salaries, and locations-this project offers a steamlined, single-page interface to quickly explore crucial market trends and compensation insights.

## Skills Showcased

- **Dashboard Design:** Crafting an intuitive and cisually appealing report layout.
- **Power Query ETL:** Performing data cleaning, shaping, and trasformation.
- **Data Modeling:** Building efficient data models with relationships (Star Schema principles).
- **DAX Fundametals:** Creating calculations and aggregations to drive key insights.
- **Visualizations Utilized:**
     * **Core Charts:** Column, Bar, Line, and Area charts for comparisons and trends.
     * **Map Charts:** For displaying deospatial data.
     * **Cards:** To highlight key performance indicators.
     * **Tables:** For presenting detailed, tabular information.
     * **Chart Variety:** Selecting from common and uncommon chart types fro effective storytelling.
     * **Interactive Features**
          * **Slicers:** Enabling dynamic, user-driven data filtering.
          *  **Buttons & Bookmarks:**For streamlined navigation and managing report views (including Drill-Through).


## Dashboard Overview

This second iteration consolidates the dashboard into a **single, focused page**, designed to give job seekers the most critical market insights at their fingertips.

![Dashboard Page1](/images/Project2_Page1.PNG)

This page acts as your concise mission control for the data job market. It showcases key performance indicators (KPIs) like **Job Count, Skills Per Job, Median Yealry Salary, and Median Hourly Salary**.You can also quickly compare **Salaries across different Job Titles**, all designed for an efficient overviw.

## Conclusion

This updates dashboard (Version 2.0) showcases Power BI's ability to tranform extensive job posting data into a powerful, streamlined tool for career analysis. It empowers **JoB Seekers, Job Transitioners, and Job Swappers** by allowing them to filter and explore essential market insights efficiently on a single page, helping them make informed decisions about their next career move.
